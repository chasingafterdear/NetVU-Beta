Our Story

Get to Know Us

 

The Network of Vertafore Users (NetVU) is an independent, national member organization committed to providing industry advocacy, world-class education and networking. NetVU has a membership of more than 30,000 insurance agencies, carriers, managing general agents and compliance organizations with more than 500,000 users of Vertafore solutions. NetVU members reside in every state and are supported by more than 55 local and virtual Chapters led by NetVU members.

Ready to get started? Visit NetVU.org to learn more (call or email for registration credentials).

 

 

Featured Education Resources

Webinars

Live and on-demand webinars provide product training by Vertafore experts and fellow industry users. Additional topics include regulation, personnel, and leadership.

 

Immersion Training

These sessions combine focused instruction with hands-on learning to give users a jump-start on execution, so what is learned has a bottom-line impact on member firms.

 

Chapter Meetings

Local and virtual chapters offer opportunities to network and learn from other Vertafore users and industry experts.

 

Accelerate

The "crown jewel" of NetVU's educational offerings happens annually at our national users conference. Join us May 21-23, 2019 in Cincinnati for intense product and persona focused education.

 

Your firm is a member of NetVU (Network of Vertafore Users) if using one of these Vertafore systems:

  • AMS360
  • Vertafore Agency Platform
  • Sagitta
  • QQCatalyst
  • AIM
  • ImageRight/WorkSmart
  • RiskMatch
  • PLRating
  • FSCRater
  • ReferenceConnect
  • Sircon
  • TransactNOW

No dues are required to engage with NetVU, and members can benefit from NetVU's resources in education, networking and advocacy.
