Associate Member Benefits

NetVU associate members provide essential support to our network's education, events, and offerings. In aiding NetVU to achieve our goals, these valued collaborators develop dynamic relationships with industry professionals and increase the exposure of their organization. NetVU associate members receive a vast array of benefits including:

  • Visibility with over 30,000 insurance agencies, carriers and MGAs, and over 500,000 users of Vertafore solutions. NetVU members reside in every state and are supported by over 50 local and virtual Chapters led by NetVU members 
  • Access to member registration rates for NetVU events
  • Access to NetVU’s online community (NCOM) *must sign code of conduct prior to access
  • Subscription to NetVU e-newsletters and others communications
  • A listing on NetVU’s website
  • Opportunity to submit presentation ideas during the Call for Presentations for NetVU events. Applications subject to committee approval
  • Special recognition at NetVU events and within the Event App
  • Priority selection and member pricing for NetVU Conference Exhibit Space
  • Priority selection of event sponsorship opportunities

Our Partners

Because of its industry leadership position in providing education, networking and advocacy, NetVU has drawn sustained and broad support from insurance carriers and service providers in the industry. In addition to its support from founding partner Vertafore, NetVU has support from platinum, gold, and silver partners, which provide sponsorship funds that supplement the organization’s revenues and help to create more opportunities for education, networking and advocacy.
